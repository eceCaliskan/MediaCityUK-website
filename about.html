What is MediaCityUK ?

MediaCityUK is known as a technology centre in Manchester built in 2007. The city has been restored and became the home of outstanding public service broadcasters, BBC, ITV, networking and communication Company ERICSSON The University of Salford also has a building in MediaCityUK. Some courses, which are related to media such as Public Relations and Digital Communications and Journalism with Public Relations are teaching in the heart of the media. MediaCityUK not only a place to work or study, but also have fun and visit. There are lots of places to see such as Lowry building, Lowry Outlet and IWM North.
